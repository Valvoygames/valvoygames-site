Privacy Policy

Sorted! - The Color Card Game respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Device ID: We collect your device’s unique identifier to manage leaderboards and prevent duplicate entries.
  • Player Name & Country: You may enter a name and country for your profile. These can be fictional and are only used for display in leaderboards and stats.
  • Game Data: We store your scores, score's dates, stats, favorite theme, and mastery rank to provide game features and leaderboards. This data is not personally identifiable.
AdMob: Our app uses Google AdMob to display ads and rewarded videos. AdMob may collect device information and usage data for advertising and analytics purposes. Please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy for details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display leaderboards and player stats.
  • To save your game progress and preferences.
  • To show ads and rewarded videos via AdMob.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as required for leaderboard functionality and ad services.
Leaderboard data (name, country, scores) may be visible to other players.

Children’s Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us for removal.

Your Choices

  • You may choose any name or country for your profile.
  • You may reset your profile at any time to erase your game data.
  • You may opt out of personalized ads via your device settings.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app or on our website.
